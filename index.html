Our Donate Plan

We divide the donation into three parts. Providing food, shelter, and training for survival. Every flood-affected family is included in this plan.

Food for a family

Each family will get 25 kg rice, 5 kg masur pulses, 5 litters oil, pure drinking water, and dry food like chira, murdi, biscuits etc.

Shelter for a family

Every family affected by the floods they will be rescued and taken to safe places.

Training for everyone

Every person who is trapped in the flood will be trained. So that they can be safe and rescue others.
